Pusarla Venkata Sindhu

Pusarla Venkata Sindhu (born 5 July 1995)[5] is an Indian badminton player.Considered one of India's most successful sportspersons,has won medals
at various tournaments such as the Olympics and on the BWF circuit, a gold at the 2019 World Championships . She is the first and only Indian to
become the badminton world champion and only the second individual from India to win two consecutive medals at the Olympic Games. rose to a career-high world ranking of no. 2 in April 2017.
